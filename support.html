Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a daily entry?

Tap the "+ New Entry" button on the dashboard. Fill in any combination of weight, exercise, diet, recovery, and your body vibe score for the day. You don't need to fill in all fields.

Can I edit or delete an entry?

Yes! Tap any entry card to edit it. To delete an entry, long-press on the entry card and confirm deletion.

What is the Body Vibe Score?

The Body Vibe Score is a -2 to +2 scale representing how you feel each day. Use it to track your overall wellness, energy, and mood.

How do the tag suggestions work?

When entering diet, exercise, or recovery activities, the app learns from your previous entries and suggests common items. Just tap a suggestion to add it quickly.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! All your data is stored locally on your device only. We never collect, transmit, or access your information. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What happens if I uninstall the app?

Your data is stored locally, so uninstalling the app will delete all your entries. Make sure to export your data first if you want to keep it.

Can I export my data?

Yes! The app includes an export feature to save your entries as a backup.

What do the different time ranges show?

D (Day): Your most recent single entry
W (Week): Last 7 days average
M (Month): Last 30 days average
6M (6 Months): Last 180 days average
Y (Year): Last 365 days average
